Eva Ellsworth

About

Whatever your relationship with music looks like, I'd love to be part of it!

Multi-Instrumentalist · Producer · Audio Engineer

Hi, I'm Eva! A teaching artist, music producer, and audio engineer based in Utah. I've spent over a decade working across the full spectrum of music, from playing brass in symphony orchestras and accordion at local fairs, to engineering records at my city's highest-rated studio and composing soundtracks for video games.

I have a knack for hearing what a song wants to be and the technical skill to get it there. My roots are in classical and jazz, but I work across a wide range of genres, from grunge and folk to electronic, and I bring every project to a polished, radio-worthy quality. Whether you need a record mixed, a song produced, or a score that lands with just the right feeling, I'm here for it.

I also teach music lessons for all ages and skill levels, covering 13 instruments, production software like FL Studio, Ableton, and Cubase, and music theory. My focus is always on making complex ideas feel clear, practical, and fun.

Whatever your relationship with music looks like, I'd love to be part of it!
